Photoshop

Tasked with creating a realistic magazine cover, I chose to replicate one of my favorite publishers, ESPN.

I felt the original image of Lebron James as seen on the right had a great color scheme to build off from, and after extending the black background of the image to fill the entire page, I had what felt like a natural fit.

As for the rest, it was only a matter of properly formatting the headlines and titles in regards to importance and adding the minor details such as code and price.

This was a fun project due to the level of freedom given, where the outcome was up to the creator’s imagination. I think personally it shows my versatility in photoshop, as there are multiple effects and images clipped together into one scene.

In this specific edit, the main focus was laying one photo on top of another, but being able to let the background bleed through the image on top. There was a lot to learn with blending in photoshop to produce the unique outcome.
